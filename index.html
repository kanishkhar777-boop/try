About

Tourist Link is India's premier platform connecting travelers with authentic local tour guides. Founded in 2023, we believe that the best way to experience a destination is through the eyes of a local expert.

Our mission is to make travel more meaningful, authentic, and sustainable by supporting local communities and their tourism professionals. Every guide on our platform is verified with official authentication IDs to ensure quality and safety.

Whether you're exploring ancient temples in India, bustling markets in Southeast Asia, or historic sites in Europe, our guides will help you create memories that last a lifetime.
